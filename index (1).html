12 months out — build your target list

Research which companies in your field actually sponsor. Start conversations before you need a job. LandedUS gives you the list to start from.

Research phase
2

6–9 months out — start applying

This is the real window. Most industries front-load hiring. Waiting until January for a May graduation means you've already missed a lot of the market.

Apply now
3

90 days before graduation — file OPT

This is the earliest USCIS allows. Don't wait. Processing times vary and missing your start date has real consequences on your offer.

Critical deadline
4

March — the H-1B lottery

One window. Once a year. Your employer registers you. If you're not employed by March, this year's lottery doesn't apply to you.

Annual window
5

STEM-OPT extension — 24 more months

STEM degree holders can extend OPT by two years. File before your current OPT expires or you lose the option entirely.
