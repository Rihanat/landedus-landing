Application timing

The window is smaller than most students realise.

Most people start too late, not because they weren't ready, but because nobody gave them a clear picture of when everything needed to happen.

May 2025 graduation

Start applyingSep 2024
File OPTFeb 2025
H-1B lotteryMar 2025 ⚡
OPT startJun 2025
STEM-OPT extensionFile before May 2027
1

12 months out — build your target list

Research which companies in your field actually sponsor and start conversations before you need a job. LandedUS gives you the verified list to start from.

Preparation
2

6 to 9 months out — start applying

This is the real window. Most industries front-load their hiring so waiting until January for a May graduation means you've already missed a significant portion of the market.

Apply now
3

90 days before graduation — file your OPT

USCIS processing takes time and missing your OPT start date has real consequences on any job offer you've accepted. We send you a WhatsApp reminder so you don't have to track this yourself.

Critical deadline
4

March — the H-1B lottery opens

This window comes once a year and you need to already be employed for your employer to register you. If you're not in a role by March, this year's lottery won't apply to you.

Annual window
5

STEM-OPT extension — 24 additional months

If your degree is STEM-designated you can extend your OPT by two years. You need to file before your current OPT expires or you lose the option entirely.
